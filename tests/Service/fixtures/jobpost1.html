RPA – Automation Developer

Engineering / Financial Engineering and Fraud in Stockholm, Sweden
As a passionate and driven Robotic Process Automation Developer you will assist with the adoption and roll-out of business process automation using UiPath in Spotify. You will help free up Spotify business teams from working on mundane, repetitive tasks, allowing them to learn new technical skills and accelerating their contribution to Spotify’s growth and success by engaging in high-value activities & innovation.
You will join the Intelligent Automation Global Centre of Excellence in Stockholm – part of the Financial Engineering (FinE) mission – and work closely with stakeholders to deliver business value, at speed, from intelligent automation. You will work in a team where diversity, teamwork and collaboration reign.
You have extensive experience working with UiPath automation, have been involved at every stage of the RPA development lifecycle and understand different RPA Operating Models.
You are a ‘people person’ and will work diligently to foster a strong collaboration network throughout Spotify.

Who you are

  • Support the implementation and enablement of a Global Automation Centre of Excellence
  • Design and develop strategies and recommendations for UiPath-enabled solutions including key aspects of architecture, migration and security
  • Assist with the development and execution of a Program Roadmap including project prioritization and delivery
  • Support CoE driven automation and Citizen led development
  • Analyze, design and implement automation solutions
  • Works side by side with Business Analyst and/or SME’s for creating process documentation 
  • Setup a framework to monitor and measure the effectiveness of automation
  • Help maintain quality and organization of platform code and data
  • Work with cross functional teams to tackle complex challenges

What you’ll do

  • UiPath Developer certification
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in RPA development – UiPath a distinct advantage
  • Knowledge of at least one of the programming tools/languages (e.g., C, Web, .Net, VB, Python)
  • Experience leading the configuration, development, testing and deployment of automation solutions
  • Knowledge of OCR, functional libraries, other tools and methods to accelerate development
  • Hands-on experience with advanced automation tools (OCR, AI/ML)
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to analyze complex requirements and define technical / functional advanced solutions
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to explain the technical aspects to business people and relevant stakeholders
  • Strong business skills and experience in working with cross/functional teams e.g Security, IAM, Finance, Internal Audit
  • Experience with Agile project delivery methods, tools, and concepts
You are welcome at Spotify for who you are, no matter where you come from, what you look like, or what’s playing in your headphones. Our platform is for everyone, and so is our workplace. The more voices we have represented and amplified in our business, the more we will all thrive, contribute, and be brilliant.
