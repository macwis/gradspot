Director of Engineering – Premium R&D

Engineering / Premium Tier in Stockholm, Sweden
As the world’s largest audio streaming subscription service, Spotify Premium enables millions of creatives to make a living from their art by connecting them with the people who love it. Research & Development deep dives into user behaviour to create a more valuable and meaningful experience for subscribers. It’s here that you’ll grow our audience and our platform, working with many disciplines across many locations to build innovative and tech-powered product solutions.
Spotify Premium R&D is looking for an engineering leader to join the Premium Mission.
The POP Tribe (Premium Opportunities and Propositions) is responsible for identifying user needs across global markets and building differentiated commercial Premium product offerings to meet those needs.
This role will be accountable for building and delivering the future of Premium products and services and ensuring those experiences meet our ambitious commercial goals, driving the next phase of Spotify Premium’s meteoric growth.

What you’ll do

  • You will lead engineering for the POP tribe, a growing team with 70+ cross functional members split across our Stockholm, London and New York offices.
  • The team is responsible for building and handling the various current and future Spotify premium offerings across our global markets, including Spotify for Students, Spotify Premium Family and Spotify for Kids and more.
  • You will drive development, launch and scaling of existing offerings as well as new and upcoming ones across the globe.

You will be

  • Working in close collaboration with your peers in product, design and product insights to lead engineering and deliver extraordinary products and offerings to our paying users.
  • Accountable for the technology strategy in support of the business goals, mission and tribe product strategies.
  • Responsible for growing a healthy, collaborative engineering and product development culture in line with our valuesEnabling technical excellence and championing strategic technical bets to partners.
  • Accountable for the growth and development of the engineering function and its team members.
  • Directly leading the senior-most individual contributing engineers, staying close to the technical architecture and decision-making in our cross-BU technical steering councils
  • Expected to own and drive strategic, cross tribe engineering initiatives from time-to-time

  • You have real-world experience leading and growing engineering in organisations of 50+ people and have a consistent track record of successfully operating as a “leader of leaders”.
  • You are passionate about technology, people and the opportunity to enable and shape Spotify’s ability to provide the best product offerings possible.
  • You have experience in building, delivering and managing products or services across the full product lifecycle, from early discovery, MVP, launch, large scale build out, subsequent migrations and sunsetting.
  • You have experience working across a complex and evolving product set, working in close collaboration with product, design and product insights teams, and peers, using product discovery and hypothesis-driven product development techniques.
  • You are passionate about establishing strategies that enable teams to succeedYou have experience in hiring, coaching and growing high-performing teams across geographically and time zone dispersed locations.
  • You have strong communication skills and can navigate an ever-evolving, matrixed organisation across multiple offices/time zones where priorities are often in competition.
  • You are open to travel from time-to-time and capable of leading remote teams as effectively as those you work on-site with.
  • You understand and value bringing joy and a sense of creativity to your day-to-day
You are welcome at Spotify for who you are, no matter where you come from, what you look like, or what’s playing in your headphones. Our platform is for everyone, and so is our workplace. The more voices we have represented and amplified in our business, the more we will all thrive, contribute, and be brilliant. So bring us your personal experience, your perspectives, and your background. It’s in our differences that we will find the power to keep revolutionizing the way the world listens.
Spotify transformed music listening forever when we launched in 2008. Our mission is to unlock the potential of human creativity by giving a million creative artists the opportunity to live off their art and billions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by these creators. Everything we do is driven by our love for music and podcasting. Today, we are the world’s most popular audio streaming subscription service with a community of more than 258 million users.
